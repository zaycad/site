About

We specialize in graphic design, digital signage, and creative fabrication. We incoroprate a varierty of innovative manufacturing and design  technologies to bring ideas to life.   

From animated signage, video editing, CAD Designs, and 3D Printing,  ZAYCAD utilizes digital media to fulfill manufacturing needs. We specialize in one-off, novel projects.       

ZAYCAD is a black owned buisnes located in Philadelphia, PA. ZAYCAD is helmed by Isaiah Coleman. Isaiah is a graphic artist and plastic fabricator from Denver, Colorado
